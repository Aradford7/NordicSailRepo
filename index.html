How to Play Contact!
Win the game by helping your viking clan survive their raiding expedition until they reach home!
To win: Clan members can not reach 0, clan members can die in battle and starvation.
Ships can help you carry more loot that find throughout your raid!
Raid to earn gold to buy more provision from the shop to help your survival!
Once you travel to your home (1000m distance) sucessfully, you win!
